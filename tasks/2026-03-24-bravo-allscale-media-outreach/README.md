# Task #1: Find Media to Promote Bravo Rewards x AllScale Partnership

## What
Find media outlets and specific journalists to reach out to for promoting the campaign: **Bravo Rewards will support crypto top-up through AllScale**.

## Why
AllScale needs press coverage for its first consumer-facing crypto payments integration. Bravo Rewards has an existing user base (30K+) and restaurant network (300-450+) in Vancouver — this is a concrete "crypto in the real world" story that media loves.

## Context

### Bravo Rewards
- **What:** Dining credit/cashback app in Greater Vancouver. Consumers preload funds, get ~15% effective discount at partner restaurants. Restaurants get upfront cash flow.
- **Website:** [bravoup.ca](https://bravoup.ca)
- **Founder:** Max So (2x fintech founder, previously co-founded IOT Pay, acquired by GrubMarket 2022)
- **Scale:** ~300-450 restaurant partners, 30,000+ users, $2.5M raised (Celtic House Asia Partners, Hat-Trick Capital)
- **Includes:** 8 MICHELIN Guide-recommended restaurants (L'Abattoir, PiDGiN, etc.)
- **Expansion plans:** 800 BC restaurants, then Calgary, Toronto, Seattle
- **US comparable:** inKind ($100M+ raised)
- **Press:** BIV article (syndicated to 6+ BC papers), Business Leadership Podcast, scheduled Web Summit Vancouver speaker (May 30, 2026)

### The Story
AllScale (Shawn's stablecoin payments company) is powering crypto top-up for Bravo Rewards — users can convert stablecoins (e.g. USDC) to dining credits. This is a real-world crypto payments use case at Vancouver restaurants.
