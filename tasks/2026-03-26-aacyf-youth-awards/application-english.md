## English Bio (300 words max)

Shawn Pang is the Founder and CEO of AllScale, a self-custody stablecoin neobank that has raised $6.5M from Informed Ventures, Yzi Lab, Generative Ventures, Amber Group, Draper Dragon, and other leading investors. Within three months of product launch, AllScale has processed over $80M in payment transactions, building 100% non-custodial infrastructure that makes stablecoin payments as simple as traditional card payments.

AllScale recently partnered with Bravo Rewards, Vancouver's largest dining cashback platform (40,000+ users, 450+ restaurants), to launch one of the first real-world stablecoin consumer payment integrations in Canada. The company is also building payment infrastructure for AI agentic commerce, compatible with Google's AP2 and Coinbase's x402 protocols.

Before AllScale, Shawn co-founded HashMatrix, a tier-1 go-to-market agency for AI companies. He bootstrapped it from $1,000/month revenue to a team of 30 and over $5M monthly cashflow in three years. HashMatrix's clients included global tech leaders — Ant Group, Tencent, Alibaba, ByteDance — and high-growth AI companies including MiniMax, 01.ai, Manus, and the rabbit R1. Shawn sold his shares and exited HashMatrix to solve the biggest payment infrastructure problem he encountered while scaling.

Shawn was awarded Top 30 Marketers in Canada by Founders Beta. He previously served as a Product Manager at Capital One and TikTok.

Beyond his companies, Shawn co-founded ID8 Lab, one of Canada's largest Chinese founders communities, supporting cross-border entrepreneurship and connecting Chinese-Canadian tech talent.

Shawn holds a dual degree in Computer Science and Honors Business Administration from the Ivey Business School at Western University.

## Company/Organization Description

AllScale is a self-custody stablecoin neobank for global businesses. Backed by $6.5M from investors including Informed Ventures, Amber Group, and Draper Dragon, AllScale provides non-custodial infrastructure enabling merchants to accept, hold, and disburse stablecoins seamlessly. The platform has processed over $80M in transactions within three months of launch, powering real-world stablecoin payments across dining, retail, and cross-border use cases. Headquartered in Vancouver, Canada.
